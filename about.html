About

Welcome! to my Photography page

Hi I'm Monica, an 18 year old photographer. This is my photography page where I capture anything that is around me. You can look through my images under 'portfolio'. I am based in Malta and spend most of time surrounded by nature. In this website, I would show a sample of landscape photography with outstanding seascapes. I also visit a lot of gardens and I enjoy taking pictures of flowers. My recent work was photographing bees for advertisement and I am showcasing a sample. You can also find snapshots of my pets whom I share my life.

Photography allows me to explore what to capture with my lens. I see something beautiful or interesting, I want to capture it and showcase it to others.

“While there is perhaps a province in which the photograph can tell us nothing more than what we see with our own eyes, there is another in which it proves to us how little our eyes permit us to see.” Dorothea Lange.
